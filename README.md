# Feng Shui Simulator

A browser-based 2D pixel art puzzle game where you arrange furniture in a bedroom to optimize feng shui. Score up to 100 points across 8 categories including Command Position, Chi Flow, Bagua Harmony, and more.

## How to Play

- **Drag** furniture from the sidebar onto the room grid
- Press **R** while dragging to **rotate** before placing
- **Click** a placed item to select it, then **R** to rotate or **Del** to remove
- **Drag** placed items to reposition them
- Press **Check Feng Shui** to score your arrangement

## Scoring Categories (100 pts)

- **Command Position (20)** — Bed placement relative to the door
- **Chi Flow (15)** — Clear pathways and energy circulation
- **Bagua Harmony (15)** — Element placement in the right zones
- **Element Balance (10)** — Represent all five elements
- **Bed Symmetry (10)** — Matching items on both sides of the bed
- **Mirror Placement (10)** — Reflect light, not the bed
- **Plants (10)** — Greenery in the right spots
- **Clutter (10)** — Don't overcrowd the room

## Tech Stack

HTML5 Canvas + vanilla JavaScript. No dependencies. Three files:

- `index.html`
- `style.css`
- `game.js`

Open `index.html` in any browser to play.
