    Welcome to Feng Shui Simulator

    What is Feng Shui?

    Feng Shui is the ancient Chinese art of arranging space to harmonize energy flow (chi). A well-arranged bedroom promotes restful sleep, good fortune, and balanced life energy.

    Key Principles

    • Command Position — Place your bed where you can see the door but aren't directly in line with it. Never sleep with feet pointing at the door ("coffin position").
    • Chi Flow — Keep a clear path from the door to the bed. Don't block doorways. Energy needs room to circulate.
    • Bagua Zones — The room maps to a 3x3 grid of life areas (wealth, love, career, etc.). Place matching elements in each zone for harmony.
    • Five Elements — Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water. Balance all five, and place elements that nurture each other nearby.
    • Symmetry — Matching nightstands and lamps on both sides of the bed create partnership energy.
    • Mirrors — Never face a mirror toward the bed. Reflecting the window is good; facing the door is not.

    How to Play

    • Drag furniture from the sidebar onto the room grid.
    • Press R while dragging to rotate before placing.
    • Click a placed item to select it, then R to rotate or Del to remove.
    • Drag placed items to reposition them.
    • Press Check Feng Shui to score your arrangement across 8 categories.
    • Follow the tips to improve your score toward Grand Master (90+)!
